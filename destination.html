   10 Must-Visit Destinations that you can not miss!

1.Shilin


The world-famous karst geological wonder - Stone Forest Scenic Area is located in Shilin Yi Autonomous County, Kunming City, Yunnan Province, 78 kilometers away from Kunming City. The uncanny workmanship of nature has created a beautiful and magical stone forest, which is known as "God's Bonsai" and "Sculpture Museum of Nature". Formed 300 million years ago, the stone forest karst geological wonder has a vast distribution area, magnificent momentum, various types, and rich landscapes. The protected area covers an area of 350 square kilometers. Dissolution caves, exquisite and beautiful plateau lakes, cliffs and waterfalls with a drop of more than 90 meters... the strange rock shapes are lifelike, breathtaking and unforgettable. The Sani people of the Yi nationality who have lived in the Shilin area for generations are born singers and dancers. They create the art and poetry of life every moment and create the world-famous "Ashima" national culture. Its systematic language and characters, rich poetry and legends, gorgeous national costumes, fiery folk songs and dances, simple and rough wrestling competitions, passionate three-stringed dances, and unique weddings and funerals all reflect the ancient National cultural charm and regional characteristics.


2.Cangshan·Erhai


Cangshan Erhai National Nature Reserve is located in Dali Bai Autonomous Prefecture, Yunnan Province. It consists of Cangshan Mountain and Erhai Lake. It spans Dali, Erhai Lake, Yangbi and other cities and counties, with a total area of 79,700 hectares. Dali Cangshan Erhai Lake Reserve belongs to the category of natural ecosystems, and also belongs to the category of natural relics, including three types: forest ecosystem type, inland wetland and water ecosystem type, and geological relic type. It is a multi-level, multi-functional, Large-capacity comprehensive nature reserve. Its purpose is to protect the remains of ancient glaciers, the natural landscape of plateau lakes, unique fish such as bowfish, historical sites and cold mountain forests in Cangshan Mountain. There are nineteen majestic peaks in Cangshan Mountain, and the top of the mountain is covered with snow all the year round. There is a stream between every two peaks, flowing from top to bottom and flowing eastward until it flows into Erhai Lake. Erhai is known as the "pearl of the plateau". It is long and narrow, looking down from the sky, Erhai Lake looks like a crescent moon, lying quietly between Cangshan Mountain and Dali Bazi. There are also many beautiful bays in Erhai Lake, the larger ones are Haidong Bay, Duase Bay, Kanglang Bay and Shuanglang Bay. Especially the "three islands in the sea" are the most beautiful.


3.Pudacuo National Park


Pudacuo National Park is located in Shangri-La City, Diqing Prefecture, Yunnan Province, China. The park was announced on June 25, 2007. It is the first national park in mainland China. Pudacuo National Park is located in the center of the "Three Parallel Rivers" World Natural Heritage in Northwest Yunnan. The first national park in mainland China. In 2007, Pudacuo was opened to the outside world, about 19 kilometers away from Shangri-La County. . Pudacuo is a transliteration of Sanskrit, meaning boat and lake. "Mandala" or "Putuo" refers to a sacred Avalokiteshvara (Avalokitesvara). "Putuo" or "Puda" refers to Purdue sentient beings to reach the other side of the sea of suffering. The name of Pudacuo comes from the Tibetan word Bitahai. Pudacuo is a mysterious and pollution-free fairy tale world. Pudacuo National Park is mainly composed of Shudu Lake, Bitahai and Militang subalpine pasture. From 3500 meters to more than 4000 meters, as an important part of the Three Parallel Rivers World Natural Heritage, the park is also a nature reserve with a good ecological environment, including swamps, lakes, virgin forests, streams, springs and rare animals.


4.Meili Snow Mountain


Meili Snow Mountain is a huge north-south snow mountain group in the west of Deqin County, Diqing Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, with a total length of 150 kilometers and a park with a total area of 960 square kilometers. The "Three Parallel Rivers" area of Jinsha River, Lancang River and Nujiang River is the heartland of the Shangri-La tourist area and the World Natural Heritage of Three Parallel Rivers. Together with Gang Rinpoche in Tibet, Animaqing Mountain in Qinghai, and Gaduojuewo in Qinghai, they are also known as the four sacred mountains of Tibetan Buddhism. Meili Snow Mountain is called "Kawagebo Snow Mountain" in Tibetan areas. The local Tibetan people named it and endowed it with divinity. It has left traces of survival at the foot of Meili Snow Mountain for generations, and also endowed Meili with profound cultural connotations snow mountain.


5.Lugu Lake


Lugu Lake is located at the junction of Yunnan and Sichuan in the north of Yunnan Province. It is a national scenic spot, a nature reserve and a national 4A tourist attraction. Lugu Lake is one of the few virgin lakes in the world that still retains a clean and simple style. It is one of the nine plateau lakes in Yunnan, and its water body belongs to Class I water quality. The total area of Luguhu Scenic Area is 165.6 square kilometers, the total area of the lake area is 50.3 square kilometers, the lake surface is 2690 meters above sea level, the maximum water depth is 93 meters, and the average water depth is 45 meters. The Mosuo people who have lived on the banks of Lugu Lake for generations still live in a matriarchal family. The "walking marriage system in which men do not marry women" is known as the living fossil of human family history and marriage history, and has extremely high sociological value. and tourism value. The perfect combination of nature and humanities, Lugu Lake has become one of the most distinctive and potential tourist destinations in Yunnan. The scenic spot has successively won titles such as "China's Top Ten Best Ecological and Cultural Tourism Scenic Spots", "China's Top Ten Most Beautiful Lakes" and "China's Top Ten Ecologically Famous Lakes".


6.Yuanyang Terraced Fields


Yuanyang Terraced Fields, located in the south of Ailao Mountain in Yuanyang County, Honghe Prefecture, Yunnan Province, is a masterpiece left by the Hani people for generations. Yuanyang Terraced Fields is the core area of Honghe Hani Terraced Fields. [1] The Honghe Hani Terraced Fields is a wonder of terraced farming civilization jointly created by the people of various ethnic groups, mainly the Hani nationality, by taking advantage of the special geographical climate. The terraces cultivated by the Hani people in Yuanyang vary with the terrain of the mountains. Large fields are reclaimed on gentle slopes; As big as a dustpan, there are often tens of thousands of acres on one slope. The Honghe Hani Terraced Fields are located in the south of Yunnan Province and spread across the four counties of Yuanyang, Honghe, Jinping, and Lvchun in Honghe Prefecture, with a total area of about 1 million mu. Yuanyang County alone has 170,000 mu of terraced fields. The Yuanyang Rice Terraces are landscape and pastoral paintings that have been "sculpted" by the Hani people for more than 1,300 years.


7.Dali and Lijiang Ancient cities


There are many ancient cities in Yunnan, the most famous of which are the ancient cities of Dali and Lijiang. Dali has a long history and is one of the earliest cultural birthplaces in Yunnan. According to literature, the ancestors of the Bai people flourished here in the 4th century, scattering many clan tribes. The ancient city of Dali is the former site of the ancient Nanzhao Kingdom, which presents the splendid history and culture of the ancient capital of Dali for thousands of years. With the prosperity of old history and culture and the innovation of culture with the new era, this place is both new school and has a thick sense of history. Lijiang Old Town is 2416 meters above sea level and is surrounded by mountains on all sides. It was built in the late Song and early Yuan dynasties, and flourished in the Ming and Qing dynasties, with a history of more than 800 years. The city is built on the mountain, the street is made by the water, the tiled houses are lined up, the market is thick, the families are flowing, the households are weeping poplar, a street and a scene can be painted. It is the centralized exhibition area of diversified culture and residential architecture of various ethnic groups, and is a famous cultural city that continues a strong historical lineage.


8.Puzhehei


"Puzhehei" is a Yi language, which means "a lake full of fish and shrimp", and is known as "a rare and unique karst landscape in China". It is well-known because it was filmed here in Hunan Satellite TV's parent-child program "Where Are We Going, Dad" in 2013. It is a national-level scenic spot, a national AAAAA-level tourist area, and a national wetland park. The total area is 388 square kilometers, and the core scenic spot is 15 square kilometers. It belongs to the karst area in southeastern Yunnan and is a typical karst karst landform. Known for the landscape. There are not only the beauty of Guilin's lonely peaks, clear streams, secluded caves, and strange rocks, but also the quaint charm of small bridges and flowing water houses in the south of the Yangtze River, as well as the sparkling beauty of Hangzhou West Lake, and even more magnificent than the lotus lake. Acre lotus. The rivers and lakes are densely covered, and the winding waterways surround the vast green hills. The ten thousand mu lotus blooms in elegant pink in summer, filling the whole lake. Occasionally, boats across the water will cause ripples in the lake water, scratching the surface of the lake. The blue sky, white clouds and distant mountains in the water attract the fish and shrimps jumping happily in the water. There are 265 scenic spots in the scenic area, each with its own merits, 312 lonely peaks dotted with dots, 83 karst caves in various poses, 54 lakes connected with each other, and 20,000 mu of water surface. Clear and transparent, the 13-kilometer grand canyon is majestic and majestic, the 3-kilometer tea-horse ancient road is mysterious and simple, and the 40,000-mu plateau karst wetland is breathtaking. This is Puzhehei. The simple Yi villagers, interesting folk festivals, ancient villages, cave landscapes, and pastoral scenery make this place another paradise on the land of Yunnan.


9.Dongchuan Red Land


"Dongchuan Red Land" is located in the north-east of Kunming city, and belongs to Dongchuan district of Kunming. The journey from Kunming to the center of the site is about 250 kilometers by car, and the main attractions are between 1800 and 2600 above sea level. Due to the red soil developed under the high temperature and rain in Dongchuan, Yunnan, this soil contains more iron and aluminum components, less organic matter, strong acidity, and heavy clay soil. The area of nearly 100 miles in circumference here is the most concentrated, typical and characteristic red soil on the red soil plateau of Yunnan. Lined with blue sky, white clouds and that unpredictable light, it constitutes a spectacular view of the red land. In the scenic spot grows a sand pine tree, a genus of fir in the pine family, which is said to be thousands of years old and has withered for three years before spitting new shoots. It is said to be a once-in-a-decade auspicious phenomenon.


10. Starlight night market


On the border of China's beautiful Xishuangbanna, there is an exotic starlight night market on the banks of the Lancang River, which combines the customs of the Dai people in Xishuangbanna and the exotic customs of neighboring countries. This is the most distinctive large-scale night market in the Golden Triangle area. Starlight Night Market is located inside Gaozhuang Xishuang Scenic Area, Jinghong City, Xishuangbanna. Blooming green light. When night falls, the whole night market is as brilliant as a river of stars, and the stars are twinkling. As the name suggests, it is called the Starlight Night Market. The Starlight Night Market opens at 7 o'clock every night, bypassing the Shwedagon Pagoda and walking down the stairs for 50 meters to the Starlight Night Market. There are hundreds of stalls with different characteristics, not only the delicacy of the Dai people in Xishuangbanna, but also the delicacies of neighboring countries such as Laos and Thailand, and hundreds of different kinds of snacks. In addition to the delicious food, the traditional song and dance performances of different styles brought by different ethnic groups such as Dai, Blang, and Thai are performed regularly here every night. The diners who come here can enjoy the food while admiring the sights that are rarely seen on weekdays. national performance.
