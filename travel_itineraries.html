Recommended Itineraries

choose a travel line

Day 1 : Dali

Morning:

  Arrive in Dali and check into hotel

Afternoon:

  Visit Dali Old Town and explore the local markets

Evening:

  Enjoy a relaxing evening at a local bar or restaurant

Day 2 : Dali

Morning:

  Take a boat trip to Erhai Lake and visit the Bai people's fishing village

Afternoon:

  Visit the Three Pagodas and the Chongsheng Temple

Evening:

  Take a stroll along Foreigner Street and enjoy the local nightlife

Day 3 : Lijiang

Morning:

  Take a high-speed train from Dali to Lijiang and check into hotel

Afternoon:

  Visit the Lijiang Old Town and explore the ancient architecture

Evening:

  Attend the Naxi Orchestra performance and enjoy dinner at a local restaurant
