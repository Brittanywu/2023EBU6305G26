Whether you're visiting Yunnan for a few days or a few weeks, you'll find ideas here to help you plan your trip.


Recommended Itineraries

choose a travel line

Dali

Three Pagodas:

  A complex of three ancient Buddhist pagodas standing by the Cangshan Mountains.

Erhai Lake:

   A large freshwater lake that offers scenic views and is a popular spot for boating and fishing.

Old Town of Dali:

  A historic walled city with traditional Bai architecture, bustling markets, and vibrant nightlife.

Pu'er

Jingmai Mountain:

  A scenic mountainous area that produces high-quality Pu'er tea.

Banpo Tea Garden:

  A picturesque tea garden located in the Lancang River valley.

Simao Ancient City:

  An ancient town with well-preserved Ming and Qing dynasty architecture and a tea market.

Shangri-La

Pudacuo National Park:

  A stunning nature reserve with pristine lakes, forests, and mountains.

Songzanlin Monastery:

   A Tibetan Buddhist monastery known as the "Little Potala Palace" for its grand architecture and religious significance.

Dukezong Ancient Town:

  A historic town with traditional Tibetan houses, narrow streets, and a famous tower offering panoramic views of the city.
